About Us

Welcome to Agri Deal, a cutting-edge platform that transforms the landscape of contract-based farming by connecting farmers directly with customers. Our goal is to create a fair, transparent, and efficient marketplace where farmers can secure the best prices for their products through a secure and seamless bidding and contract process.

What We Do

At Agri Deal, we simplify the farming transaction process by offering a platform where:

  • Farmers Post Products: Farmers can list their products before or after harvest, providing essential details such as expected yield, type of product, and availability.
  • Bidding Process: Multiple customers place bids on the listed products, enabling farmers to choose the most competitive offer.
  • Contract Finalization: Once a bid is selected, farmers and customers can negotiate the terms, finalizing the contract to ensure both parties are satisfied.

  • Delivery and Payment: Upon successful delivery of the products, the funds are released from escrow to the farmer, ensuring a smooth and risk-free transaction.

Why Choose Agri Deal?

  • Secure Payments with Escrow: Our escrow system ensures that both parties are protected, with funds securely held until the contract terms are fulfilled.
  • Competitive Bidding: Farmers receive bids from multiple customers, enabling them to secure the best possible deal for their products.
  • Flexible Posting: Farmers can list products even before harvest, offering them a chance to secure contracts early and plan their sales in advance.
  • Trust and Transparency: We provide real-time updates, so both farmers and customers are always informed about the bidding, contract, and delivery status.
